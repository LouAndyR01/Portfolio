About Me

My name is Louis Rodriguez, I am a Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp student through Southern Methodist University in Dallas, Texas. I was Born and raised in Dallas, with a small stint in Austin.

I have been in the mortgage industry for 14 years and now looking to make a transition to programming to challenge myself. I feel like I have learned quite a bit of coding through the class so far, but I know I have only seen the tip of the iceberg.

When I am not in front of a computer coding or drawing up numbers for clients, I have quite a few hobbies. My favorite hobbies include hiking, camping, going to music shows and most of all, traveling and learning about other cultures.

My highest hike to date is Longs Peak in Estes Park, Colorado. It is my only 15'er up to date, and I have several long hikes under my belt. My favorite hike so far has been the Grand Canyon, but I have plans to visit Zion and Yosemite this year. My ulitmate goal it to hike the Pacific Crest Trail or the Appalachian Trail one season.

My goal after I complete my bootcamp studies is to begin a career in programming that will allow me to satisfy one of my other passions, traveling.
